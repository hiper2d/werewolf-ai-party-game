There are two teams: villagers and werewolf. Villagers don't know each other, but werewolves do. There are two phases (
day and night) which go one after another until one of the teams win. Villagers win when all the werewolves are dead.
Werewolves win when they outnumber villagers. We start from a day. The day consist of a discussion between all alive
players with the goal to prepare to the end-of-the-day voting. Players can say anything, there are no limitations - it
doesn't have to be truth, nobody has to believe you. After certain amount of messages, the human player can trigger the
voting. The voting can be triggered automatically when the day discussion reaches the certain limit. Each alive player
votes to exactly one other alive player. The order is arbitrary but fixed, noone is allowed to skip and hat to vote when
its their turn. The winner dies. In case of a tie, a game master decides who dies randomly. The killed player reveals
their role, and the game continues to the Night phase. During the night, werewolves and villagers with special roles can
take their turns in the predefined order:

1. Maniac (villager). Maniac pick any other alive player, this target player is abducted for the night. The abducted
   player cannot do any actions and cannot be targeted by other players this night (all attempts to do so result as
   unsuccessful in the end of the night). However, in case of the maniac death during this night, their victim (the
   abducted player) also dies. If the maniac abducts a werewolf, this has no effect, unless it was the last alive
   werewolf. In this case, the last werewolf skips the turn.
2. Werewolves. They have a little private chat (2 messages each), and then decide who to target this night. If nobody
   saves the target player, they die.
3. Doctor. Doctor picks a target among alive players and decides if they want to heal to use their one-per-game ability
   and kill the target. The doctor cannot name the same target two nights in a row. The doctor can heal themselves (but
   not two nights in the row). The healed player cannot die this night for whatever reason.
4. Detective. Detective picks a target among alive players and decide whether this player is going to be checked or
   killed. If former, then the game master will announce in the night results if the detective checked good or bad
   player without revealing the target name or giving any hints. All villagers except the maniac are good. All
   werewolves and the maniac are bad.

After all roles perform actions, the game master calculates the night results and posts who died and why, what each role
did on the high-level. Then the next day begins and the game continues.
